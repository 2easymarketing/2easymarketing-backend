Quick-Start Guide

1
Create Business Accounts
Set up ad accounts on each platform where you want to run campaigns (Meta Business, Google Ads, TikTok for Business, etc.)
2
Get API Credentials
Each platform has a developer portal. Follow the links above to generate your access tokens and IDs.
3
Paste Credentials & Save
Enter your credentials for each platform above and click Save. Green dot = connected and ready to launch.
4
Create & Launch Campaigns
Go to Create Campaign, select your platform, fill in the brief, and hit Generate. AI builds your campaign instantly.
5
Monitor All Platforms
Live Campaigns tab shows all campaigns across all platforms in one dashboard. Pause, resume, or delete from here.
